Paper

What recycles:

  • Office papers;
  • cardboard;
  • general boxes;
  • newspapers;
  • magazines;
  • books;
  • phone directories;
  • notebooks;
  • paper card;
  • cardboards;
  • long life packaging;

What does not recycle:

  • Carbon paper;
  • cellophane;
  • vegetal paper;
  • thermofax;
  • waxed or plastified paper;
  • toilet paper;
  • Kleenex;
  • napkins;
  • photos;
  • tapes or adhesive labels.
