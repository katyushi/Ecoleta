How to Recycle

1- Know what residues you can recycle.

2- Locate a recycling center near your location.

3- Understand how selective collection works at your location. depending on the location the separation is more or less specific. Therefore, organize specific dumps.

4- Check out the glasses and plastics. Some of them may display a logo with a numbering that indicates their type. Check local regulations to see which ones are accepted and whether or not they need to be separated from each other.

5- Clean the items before recycling. it is important to rinse the food or beverage waste before separating the items into the trash. Cleaning materials facilitates recycling and helps to prevent them from generating unpleasant odors in your trash. Another good point is that clean recycling does not attract insects.
