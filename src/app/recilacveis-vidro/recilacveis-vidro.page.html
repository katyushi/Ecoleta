Glass

What recycles:

  • Bottles of Beverage;
  • jars in general;
  • jars of food products;
  • cups.

What does not recycle:

  • Mirrors;
  • crystals;
  • window glass;
  • car windows;
  • lamps*;
  • ampoules of medicines;
  • ceramics;
  • porcelain;
  • TV and computer tubes.




* Ideally, this material should be packed and disposed of in places that lead to decontamination.
